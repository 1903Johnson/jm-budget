How to install the app on your phone

⚠️ Why can't I install directly?
PWA apps must be served over HTTPS (a secure website). An HTML file opened from your phone's storage won't work. You need to host it online first — it's free and takes 3 minutes.

Step 1 — Host the App Online (Free)

🚀 Method A — Netlify Drop Easiest · Recommended

No account needed. Drag and drop. Done in 60 seconds.

1
On your computer, unzip the file JM_Budget_App_v2.zip — you get a folder called jmapp
2
Open netlify.com/drop in Chrome on your computer
3
Drag the entire jmapp folder into the dotted drop zone on that page
4
Netlify gives you a free HTTPS link like:
https://magical-name-123456.netlify.app
5
Open that link on both phones — then follow Step 2 below
Open Netlify Drop →

🔥 Method B — Firebase Hosting Best for sync users

If you're already setting up Firebase for real-time sync, host here too.

1
Install Node.js from nodejs.org on your computer
2
Open Terminal / Command Prompt and run:
npm install -g firebase-tools
3
Run firebase login → sign in with your Google account
4
Go into the jmapp folder: cd jmapp
5
Run firebase init hosting → select your JMBudget project → set public directory to . (dot) → No to SPA rewrite
6
Run firebase deploy → your app is live at yourproject.web.app

Step 2 — Install on Android & iPhone

🤖 Android
🍎 iPhone
1
Open Google Chrome on your Android phone
2
Go to your Netlify / Firebase URL (the HTTPS link from Step 1)
3
Wait a few seconds — Chrome will show an install banner at the bottom saying "Add JM Budget to Home screen" — tap it
4
If no banner appears: Tap the 3-dot menu ⋮ in top right → tap "Add to Home screen" → tap Add
5
The app appears on your home screen with the blue J&M icon — tap it to open like a real app!
✅ Both Johnson and Melba should open the same URL and install it. Once Firebase is connected, any entry either of you makes appears on the other phone within seconds.
1
Open Safari on your iPhone (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2
Go to your Netlify / Firebase URL (the HTTPS link from Step 1)
3
Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari (the square with an arrow pointing up ↑)
4
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
5
Change the name to "JM Budget" if you want, then tap Add
6
The blue J&M icon appears on your home screen — it opens full screen like a native app!

Drag the jmapp folder to netlify.com/drop on your computer → open the free HTTPS link on both phones → tap "Add to Home Screen" → done! ✅
